This is David
Goggins

Retired Navy SEAL and the only member of the U.S. Armed
Forces to complete SEAL training, Army Ranger School, and
Air Force Tactical Air Controller training. The author of the
New York times bestselling books 'Can't Hurt Me' and 'Never
Finished'.

Some facts about David Goggins!

Only Navy Seal to complete three hell weeks in one year.

Completed more than seventy ultra-distance races, often placing in the top-five.

Former Guinness World Record holder for completing 4,030 pull-ups in seventeen hours.

During the summer he works as a wildland firefighter in British Columbia.

"It's a lot more than mind over matter. It takes relentless self-discipline to schedule suffering into your day, every day."

- David Goggins
