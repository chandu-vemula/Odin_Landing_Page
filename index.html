Some facts about David Goggins!

Only member of the U.S. Armed Forces to complete SEAL training, Army Ranger School, and Air Force Tactical Air Controller training.

Goggins has completed more than seventy ultra-distance races, often placing in the top-five

Former Guinness World Record holder for completing 4,030 pull-ups in seventeen hours

During the summer he works as a wildland firefighter in British Columbia.
